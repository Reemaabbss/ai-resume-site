Cover Letter Guide

A good cover letter is not a biography—it’s a pitch. Hook them with one relevant achievement, connect your skills to the job, and show you’ve read the description.

Write 2 short paragraphs and a brief bullet list. Close with a clear ask: “I’d love to discuss how I can help improve your frontend performance.”

  • Mention the role and company by name.
  • Mirror keywords from the job post naturally.
  • End with availability and a polite call to action.
